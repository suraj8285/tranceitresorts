About Us

About Us

The Wise Oak Retreat – Managed by YOLO Hospitalities We are a professionally managed boutique homestay and resort where each stay is designed to feel slow, calm and meaningful. Our core belief is simple:.

Inspired by the Wisdom of the Oak Tree

Inspired by the strength, stability, and wisdom of the oak tree, our retreat is thoughtfully designed to create harmony between nature and mindful living.

Nature-Friendly Construction

Our cottages and spaces are built on elevated platforms, ensuring that surrounding trees and natural landscapes remain untouched, preserving the purity of the environment.

Aesthetic & Mindful Living Spaces

Every corner is designed with artistic details and calming aesthetics, encouraging slow living, reflection, and a deeper connection with your surroundings.

Peace, Culture & Creativity

Surrounded by tranquility, our retreat is ideal for meditation, reading, remote work, and creative pursuits, enriched with subtle Kumaoni cultural elements in architecture, hospitality, and food.
